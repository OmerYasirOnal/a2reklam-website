# A2 Reklam — Tabela ve Reklam Sistemleri
# Istanbul, Turkey — Since 2005

## About
A2 Reklam is a professional signage manufacturing company based in Kagithane, Istanbul.
We provide end-to-end signage solutions: design, production, installation, and maintenance.
Serving all 39 districts of Istanbul with 20+ years of experience.

## Services
- Isikli Tabela (Illuminated Signs): LED backlit channel letters, lightbox signs
- Isiksiz Tabela (Non-illuminated Signs): Dibond, composite panel signs
- Paslanmaz Harf (Stainless Steel Letters): Brushed, polished, painted metal letters
- Kutu Harf (Channel Letters): 3D fabricated letter signs with LED illumination
- Totem Tabela (Totem/Pylon Signs): Freestanding directional and brand signs
- Cephe Tabela (Facade Signs): Building-mounted commercial signage
- Cephe Giydirme (Facade Cladding): Aluminum composite panel cladding
- Arac Giydirme (Vehicle Wraps): Full/partial fleet and vehicle wrapping
- Yonlendirme (Wayfinding): Interior and exterior directional sign systems
- Kabartma Pleksiglass (Raised Plexiglass): Thermoformed acrylic signs
- Ofis Kumlama Folyolari (Office Window Films): Frosted and decorative films
- Fener Tabela (Projecting Signs): Double-sided blade/flag signs
- Cati Tabelasi (Roof Signs): Large-format rooftop signage
- Kapi Isimlik (Door Nameplates): Office and building door signs
- Ayakli A Pano (A-Frame Stands): Portable pavement signs
- Banko Uygulamalari (Counter Applications): Reception and POS displays
