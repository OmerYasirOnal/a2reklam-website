## Vehicle Wrapping: Your Mobile Billboard

Vehicle wrapping transforms your vehicle into a moving billboard, an effective marketing method. Whether you have fleet vehicles or a single commercial vehicle, professional car wrapping lets you take your brand everywhere.

### Advantages of Vehicle Wrapping

**Cost Effectiveness:**
- Years of advertising with one-time investment
- Low unit cost compared to traditional advertising
- Direct reach to target audience

**Wide Reach:**
- Thousands of views per day
- Visibility in different regions
- 24/7 passive advertising

### Vinyl Types

**Cast Vinyl:**
- Premium quality
- 7-10 year lifespan
- Conforms to curved surfaces
- Higher cost

**Calendered Vinyl:**
- Economical option
- 3-5 year lifespan
- Ideal for flat surfaces
- Mid-segment

| Vinyl Type | Lifespan | Cost | Use |
|------------|----------|------|-----|
| Cast (Premium) | 7-10 years | $$$$$ | Full wrap |
| Calendered | 3-5 years | $$$ | Partial wrap |
| Polymeric | 5-7 years | $$$$ | General use |

### Wrap Types

**Full Wrap:**
- Entire vehicle is wrapped
- Maximum impact
- Protects original paint
- Highest cost

**Partial Wrap:**
- Specific areas wrapped
- Doors, hood, trunk, etc.
- Medium budget
- Effective results

**Lettering and Logo:**
- Brand elements only
- Most economical
- Quick application

### Design Tips

**Readability:**
- Large and clear fonts
- High contrast colors
- Minimum text, maximum impact

**Contact Information:**
- Phone number mandatory
- Website address
- Social media (icons)

> **Pro Tip:** Always test your design on a car template. Door handles, windows, and curves affect appearance.

### Application Process

1. **Measurement and Template:** Vehicle dimensions taken
2. **Design:** Digital mockup prepared
3. **Approval:** Customer approves design
4. **Printing:** High-resolution vinyl printing
5. **Lamination:** UV protection layer
6. **Application:** Professional installation (1-3 days)
7. **Inspection:** Quality control and delivery

### Maintenance Tips

**Do:**
- Regular hand washing (soft cloth)
- Park in shade
- Be careful with delicate parts

**Don't:**
- Car wash machine (first 2 weeks)
- Pressure washing (away from edges)
- Abrasive chemicals

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does vehicle wrapping damage paint?**
No, quite the opposite - it protects. However, professional removal is essential. Improper removal can cause damage.

**Can a rental vehicle be wrapped?**
Possible since wrapping is temporary. But check your rental agreement.

**How long does wrapping take?**
Full wrap 2-3 days, partial wrap 1 day, lettering/logo same day.
