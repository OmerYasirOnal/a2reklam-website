## LED and Neon Signs: Factors Determining Price

The most common question from those wanting illuminated signage is "How much does it cost?" However, sign pricing cannot be expressed in a single figure; it depends on many variables. In this guide, we examine 8 key factors affecting LED and neon sign costs in detail.

### 1. Sign Dimensions

Size is the most determining factor. As size increases:
- Material quantity increases
- Number of LED modules increases
- Installation becomes more difficult
- Transportation costs rise

| Size Category | Typical Use | Price Range* |
|---------------|-------------|--------------|
| Small (1-3 m²) | Boutique, cafe | $$ |
| Medium (3-10 m²) | Store, office | $$$ |
| Large (10-30 m²) | Mall, plaza | $$$$ |
| Very large (30+ m²) | Rooftop, building facade | $$$$$ |

*Prices are indicative, contact us for current quotes.

### 2. Lighting Technology

**LED Module:**
- Most common choice
- Energy efficient (60-80% less consumption)
- 50,000+ hour lifespan
- Low maintenance cost

**Neon Glass:**
- Classic, nostalgic look
- Special color options
- Higher energy consumption
- Requires expert maintenance

**Flex Neon (LED Neon):**
- Neon look, LED advantages
- Break resistant
- Mid-price segment

### 3. Material Quality

**Body Materials:**
- Aluminum composite (economical)
- Stainless steel (premium)
- Galvanized sheet (industrial)
- Plexiglass/acrylic (light, transparent)

### 4. Design Complexity

- Simple text/logo: Standard price
- Custom fonts: +10-20%
- 3D raised effect: +30-50%
- Animated LED: +50-100%
- Multi-color RGB system: +40-60%

### 5. Installation Conditions

Factors affecting installation price:
- Height (each floor +10-15%)
- Access difficulty
- Crane/platform requirements
- Distance from electrical infrastructure

### 6. Brand and Warranty

Premium brands:
- Samsung, Cree, Osram LED chips
- 5-year warranty
- Certified power supplies

### 7. Quantity and Project Size

- Single sign: Standard unit price
- 3-5 units: 5-10% discount
- 10+ units (chain stores): 15-25% discount

### 8. Urgency and Delivery Time

- Standard (15-20 days): Normal price
- Fast (7-10 days): +20-30%
- Urgent (3-5 days): +50-75%

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much electricity does an LED sign use?**
An average store sign (5m²) consumes 30-60 kWh monthly.

**Are neon signs still being made?**
Yes, especially for retro-concept venues, bars, and boutique hotels. However, LED neon (flex neon) is more popular due to maintenance costs.
