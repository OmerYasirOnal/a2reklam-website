## What is Rooftop Signage?

Rooftop signage is a large-scale advertising solution mounted on the highest point of buildings, making your brand visible from kilometers away. Particularly preferred on main arterials with heavy traffic, industrial zones, and commercial centers, rooftop signs are a powerful visual communication tool that reflects your brand's prestige.

In crowded metropolises like Istanbul, rooftop signage is a critical investment to stand out among thousands of businesses. A well-designed and professionally installed rooftop sign promotes your brand 24/7.

### Key Benefits of Rooftop Signage

1. **Maximum Visibility:** Attracts attention even from long distances due to height
2. **24/7 Advertising:** Continuous promotion with LED lighting at night
3. **Prestige:** Strengthens your corporate identity
4. **Longevity:** Can last 15-20 years with proper material selection
5. **Local SEO Support:** Your physical presence strengthens on Google Maps

> **Pro Tip:** You can save energy by controlling your rooftop sign's LED lighting with timers.

### Material Options

| Material | Advantages | Disadvantages | Est. Lifespan |
|----------|------------|---------------|---------------|
| Stainless Steel | Durable, prestigious look | High cost | 20+ years |
| Aluminum Composite | Lightweight, economical | Impact sensitive | 15-20 years |
| Galvanized | Affordable | Requires periodic maintenance | 10-15 years |

### Lighting Types

- **LED Module:** Most common choice, energy efficient
- **Neon:** Retro look, for special applications
- **Backlit:** Homogeneous light distribution
- **Frontlit:** Classic, economical solution

### Installation Process

1. **Survey and Measurement:** Building structure and wind load analysis
2. **Design:** 3D visualization and approval
3. **Manufacturing:** Controlled production in workshop (7-15 days)
4. **Installation:** Professional mounting with cranes and safety equipment
5. **Electrical Connection:** Safe installation with licensed electrician
6. **Testing:** Lighting and durability checks

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is a municipal permit required for rooftop signage?**
Yes, rooftop sign installation in Istanbul requires an advertising license from the relevant municipality. At A2 Advertising, we guide you through this process.

**How long does rooftop signage take to complete?**
After design approval, production takes 7-15 days, and installation takes 1-3 days. Total time varies depending on project complexity.

**How much energy does an LED rooftop sign consume?**
With modern LED systems, a 50m² sign consumes an average of 200-400 kWh per month. This can be reduced further with sensor systems.
